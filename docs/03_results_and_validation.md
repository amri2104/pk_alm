## Validation Approach

1. **Schema validation**
   - All cashflows must match the CashflowRecord schema and sign convention.

2. **Formula checks**
   - BVG formulas, cohort projections, and valuation logic are unit tested
     with manually checkable examples.

3. **Pipeline checks**
   - End-to-end tests validate that the Stage-1 baseline produces the exact
     output structure and preserves protected outputs.

4. **Plausibility anchors**
   - Funding ratios, asset allocations, rentnerquote, and income yields are
     compared to Complementa-style ranges for directional validation.
   - These checks are contextual indicators, not calibration proofs.

## Result Interpretation

- Stage-1 results are a transparent baseline, not a calibrated pension fund.
- Full ALM outputs show the integration of BVG and ACTUS cashflows and are
  reporting artifacts separate from Stage-1 outputs.
- Stochastic outputs represent rate sensitivity only. They are diagnostics,
  not a full stochastic ALM simulation.

## Interpretation Risks

- The liability proxy excludes technical reserves and Ueberobligatorium.
- Closed-population dynamics can distort long-horizon funding paths.
- Equity, real estate, and alternatives are proxy models.
- The public AAL server does not emit all asset income events.
- Stochastic results depend on rate-model assumptions and shared-seed paths.
